# ReadWise Carousel (6 Slides)

Use this exact sequence for your LinkedIn carousel.

## Slide 1

Title: `ReadWise: Mood-Based Book Recommender`  
Supporting line: `From emotion input to personalized reading suggestions.`  
Visual: Product hero screenshot (home page).

## Slide 2

Title: `Problem`  
Body: `People struggle to find books that match how they feel right now.`  
Visual: Search or browsing screen with short callout annotation.

## Slide 3

Title: `Solution Flow`  
Body: `mood input -> sentiment analysis -> personalized recommendations`  
Visual: Recommendation screen with a simple 3-step arrow overlay.

## Slide 4

Title: `Tech Stack`  
Body: `Django, DRF, Google Books API, Open Library API, Render`  
Visual: Architecture-style card or clean stack badges.

## Slide 5

Title: `Demo Screens`  
Body: `Home, recommendations, search, profile/auth`  
Visual: 2x2 grid of screenshots.

## Slide 6

Title: `Call to Action`  
Body: `Need a similar app? DM for collaboration (US/Canada/Dubai).`  
Footer: `Live demo + GitHub link in post caption.`  
Visual: Clean branded closing slide.

## Slide Design Constraints

- Keep body text <= 2 lines per slide.
- Minimum font size: 28 pt for body, 44 pt for titles.
- Use high contrast text/background.
- Keep margins consistent so it is readable on mobile.
